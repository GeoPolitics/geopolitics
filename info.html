Rules For GeoPolitics

1. Read The Rules (This includes Staff)

2. Don’t use ad hominems during debates

3. Don’t threaten other members (this includes doxxing)

4. Following discord TOS is mandatory

5. Don’t meme during a serious debate

6. No nsfw in non-nsfw channels (including Gore)

7. Any drama between individuals in other servers is not of our concern; don't bring it into debates here

8. Autonomous debates will be permitted, but official debates moderated by judges will be logged

9. You’ll get 3 warnings until a kick or ban depend on how severe the rules you broke were (take these 3 warnings to correct yourself or get banned/kicked)

10. Don’t react to polls unless it’s a shrug or think

11. Don’t @everyone unless you're council (don’t constantly @ people)

12. Don’t spam

13. Don’t ping roles unless it’s for debates

14. Not all roles are in #roles so if you want a custom one ask the mod or staff team

15. Don’t ban or kick people for no reason, don’t make useless announcements, and don’t change the logo if the owner doesn’t approve.

16. Block people if you really don’t wanna debate them and they keep insisting.

17. 2 pings per day max unless it’s actually important and that goes for polls too.

18. No sh*tposting in the important & politics category.

19. Try to speak English if you can.

20. Don’t advertise servers (even in dms) unless the council approves.

21. Controversial topics need approval from a staff member to get debated (if the guard or council member is spectating or debating along you won’t need to ask)

22. Use the correct channels ALWAYS!

23. Don’t post any sexual/revealing photos of minors.

24. No slurs or discriminatory words in serious debates
